Improvement Tips
→Use at least 12 characters — length is the single biggest factor in password security.
→Mix uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols to expand your charset significantly.
→Avoid dictionary words or predictable patterns like "Password1!" even with substitutions.
→Consider a passphrase — four random words joined together are both memorable and strong.
→Never reuse passwords across sites. Use a password manager to store unique credentials.
